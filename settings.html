About

QR PDF & Compressor v1.0

This app provides a QR scanner, PDF converter, and image compressor all in one PWA. Local-first tool, no back end required.

Help

  • Use the QR scanner to scan codes at businesses or online.
  • Convert supported files into PDFs using the PDF converter.
  • Compress images to save storage and speed up uploads.

This PWA is a sample local-first tool. No back end required.
